Reversi

The game

Reversi (a.k.a. Othello) is a game based on a grid with eight rows and eight columns, played between you and the computer, by adding pieces with two sides: black and white.

At the beginning of the game there are 4 pieces in the grid, the player with the black pieces is the first one to place his piece on the board.

Each player must place a piece in a position that there exists at least one straight (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) line between the new piece and another piece of the same color, with one or more contiguous opposite pieces between them.

The program

When you start the Reversi program, you must do the first move as black player, then the computer will make the next move.

The menu of the program gives you the following possibilities;
  • Game - New: new board
  • Game - Undo: undo the last move
  • Game - Hint: the computer will move on behalf of you
  • Game - Level: select the level of game, between 2 (easy) and 6 (difficult)
  • Game - Theme: select your favorite theme
